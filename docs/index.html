Board Game

A board game akin to scrabble with simpler rules.

Showcasing a Haskell game server and a React drag and drop UI. Currently in version 0.2 alpha trial.

Check it out! Drag and drop letters to form a word connected to an existing tile on the board and click Commit Play to play that word.

Hosting: thanks to oceanpark.com.

Please add any issues you find and ideas for improvments to the issues list.

Rules

The board game is not intended to closely mimic scrabble. The rules are simpler, and may evolve over time, but not necessarily in the direction of scrabble. Here are a few differences.
  • Scoring is simplified: the score for a play is the total length of the word constructed in the principle direction of the play (horizonatl or vertical).
  • Tiles are selected randomly with replacement (unlike scrabble in which tiles are selected randomly without replacement). The probability distribution of letters is not necessarily that of scrabble.
  • Cross words are not checked - only the word in the principle direction. This is a known issue.
  • A game needs to be explicitly ended by the user.
  • Games have a time limit of 30 minutes. Longer games are considered abandoned and are harvested.
